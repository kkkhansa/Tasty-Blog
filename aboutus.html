About Tasty Blog

 

A recipe is a formula of ingredients and a list of instructions for creating prepared foods.
It is used to control quality, quantity, and food costs in a foodservice operation. A recipe may be simple to complex based on the requirements of the operation and the intended user. For example, an experienced chef may need a recipe with only a few details, while a beginner cook may need more information about ingredients, preparation steps, cooking times and temperatures, visual cues, and equipment requirements.
With Tasty Blog you can easily browse for recipe! Our Mission is to make cooking easier for you!
